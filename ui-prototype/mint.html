FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about minting your CONTRA identity.

What happens to my staked USDC?

+

Your staked USDC remains in your custody within the CONTRA protocol's smart contract. It is never locked — you can unstake at any time with a 7-day cooldown period. While staked, you earn yield through the protocol's revenue-sharing mechanism and DeFi integrations. Your principal is always protected by audited smart contracts and multi-sig governance.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

+

Absolutely. You can upgrade your tier at any time by staking the difference in USDC. For example, moving from Officer (10,000 USDC) to Partner (15,000 USDC) requires staking an additional 5,000 USDC. Your benefits upgrade instantly upon confirmation. Downgrading is also supported — simply unstake the excess amount.

How does revenue sharing work?

+

Partner and Strategist tier holders receive a share of CONTRA's platform fees proportional to their stake relative to the total Partner+ pool. Revenue is distributed monthly in USDC directly to your connected wallet. The current distribution rate is approximately 8-12% APY, varying with platform volume. Strategist holders receive an additional bonus multiplier.

Is there a referral program?

+

Yes! CONTRA offers a referral program where you earn 5% of the staking amount for every new member you bring in who mints an Officer tier or above. Referral rewards are paid in USDC and are claimable immediately. Your referral link is generated after you mint your own identity — share it to grow the ecosystem and earn passive rewards.
