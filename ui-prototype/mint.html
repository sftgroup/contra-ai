FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about minting your CONTRA identity.

What is a Founding Shareholder NFT?

+

It is your permanent on-chain identity as an early backer of the CONTRA protocol. As a founding shareholder, you gain access to exclusive benefits including fee sharing, governance rights, and early access to new features.'s smart contract. It is never locked — you can unstake at any time with a 7-day cooldown period. While staked, you earn yield through the protocol's revenue-sharing mechanism and DeFi integrations. Your principal is always protected by audited smart contracts and multi-sig governance.

Can I transfer my NFT?

+

Yes, the Founding Shareholder NFT is a standard ERC-721 token and can be transferred to any address. However, some benefits (such as early access priority) may be tied to the original minter.

How do I pay for minting?

+

Minting is done with USDC (USD Coin) on your chosen blockchain. The mint price is 10,000 USDC per NFT across all 4 chains. Make sure you have enough USDC in your wallet before minting.

Are NFTs on different chains the same?

+

Yes, all Founding Shareholder NFTs have equal rights and benefits regardless of which chain you mint on. The only difference is the chain-specific token ID and contract address. Each chain has its own independent numbering.
